# Premium Screen SRS

## Overview

Premium Screen la man hinh dau tien cua ung dung, hien thi loi moi nang cap goi Premium, danh sach quyen loi, cac goi dang ky, thong tin dung thu, nut dang ky, va cac lien ket chinh sach.

## Screen States

- Trang thai mac dinh: hien thi Premium Screen voi goi `Yearly` duoc chon san.
- Trang thai chon goi: hien thi goi nguoi dung vua chon va bo chon goi truoc do.
- Trang thai mua that bai: hien thi thong bao mua that bai.

## Common Components

- Nen man hinh mau den.
- Nut dong `X` o goc tren trai.
- Anh banner lon o phia tren man hinh.
- Tieu de `BE PREMIUM`.
- Mo ta `to unlock all features`.
- Danh sach quyen loi gom `Remove Ads`, `Cast to TV`, `Unlimited Playlist & Channels`, va `Smooth & Stable Streaming`.
- Danh sach goi dang ky gom `Yearly`, `Monthly`, va `Weekly`.
- Goi `Yearly` hien thi gia `$13.99` va nhan `Best Offer`.
- Goi `Monthly` hien thi gia `$4.99`.
- Goi `Weekly` hien thi gia `$2.99`.
- Text `3-day free trial. Cancel Anytime`.
- Nut `Subscribe Now`.
- Lien ket `Privacy Policy`.
- Text `Restore`.
- Lien ket `Term of Use`.

## User Actions

| User action | System action |
|---|---|
| Mo ung dung | Hien thi `Premium Screen` la man hinh dau tien |
| Cham nut `X` | Dong `Premium Screen` va vao Home Screen |
| Cham goi `Yearly` | Chon goi `Yearly`, bo chon goi truoc do, va cap nhat trang thai chon |
| Cham goi `Monthly` | Chon goi `Monthly`, bo chon goi truoc do, va cap nhat trang thai chon |
| Cham goi `Weekly` | Chon goi `Weekly`, bo chon goi truoc do, va cap nhat trang thai chon |
| Cham nut `Subscribe Now` | Bat dau mua goi dang duoc chon |
| Mua that bai | Hien thi thong bao mua that bai |
| Cham `Restore` | Khong co hanh dong |
| Cham `Privacy Policy` | Mo trang/chinh sach quyen rieng tu |
| Cham `Term of Use` | Mo trang/dieu khoan su dung |

## Acceptance Criteria

- Premium Screen hien thi la man hinh dau tien cua ung dung.
- Premium Screen hien thi nen den, nut dong `X`, banner, tieu de `BE PREMIUM`, va mo ta `to unlock all features`.
- Premium Screen hien thi du 4 quyen loi: `Remove Ads`, `Cast to TV`, `Unlimited Playlist & Channels`, va `Smooth & Stable Streaming`.
- Premium Screen hien thi du 3 goi dang ky: `Yearly`, `Monthly`, va `Weekly`.
- Goi `Yearly` hien thi gia `$13.99`, duoc chon mac dinh, va hien thi nhan `Best Offer`.
- Goi `Monthly` hien thi gia `$4.99`.
- Goi `Weekly` hien thi gia `$2.99`.
- Moi thoi diem chi co mot goi duoc chon.
- Khi nguoi dung cham goi khac, goi do duoc chon va goi truoc do bi bo chon.
- Nut `Subscribe Now` bat dau mua goi dang duoc chon.
- Khi mua that bai, he thong hien thi thong bao mua that bai.
- Cham nut `X` dong Premium Screen va vao Home Screen.
- Cham `Restore` khong co hanh dong.
- Cham `Privacy Policy` mo trang/chinh sach quyen rieng tu.
- Cham `Term of Use` mo trang/dieu khoan su dung.
- Khong co placeholder hoac trang thai mo ho.
